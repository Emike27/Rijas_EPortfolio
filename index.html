This lesson introduces students to the concept of recycling and its importance in protecting the environment. It discusses the types of recycling (internal and external) and how common materials like plastic bottles, paper plates and cups can be reused or repurposed. Through activities and discussions, learners will be able to understand how recycling helps reduce waste, conserve natural resources and know its advantages and benefits to the community.
